Soft Multi-functional materials

I have recently started exploring numerical methods for computationally efficient simulations of soft multi-functional composite materials such electroactive polymers (EAPs), Magnetoactive polymers (MAPs) and hydrogels. This is an extension of my research on novel finite element framework based on Bezier elements focussed especially at soft materials which are inherently incompressible.

Electroactive polymers - Computational Electromechanics

In collaboration with Dr Mokarram Hossain, I proposed novel Computational framework for the simulation of electroactive polymers (EAPs) Link. Our framework uses a mixed formulation and higher-order elements for accurate and efficient simulation of static and dynamic simulations of EAPs undergoing extremely large deformations.
